Our vision

Film Stalker aims to assist film lovers into getting closer to their favourite movies by making it simple to visit shooting locations and sets.

Why are we doing this?

We do this to remove the obstacles that have to be overcome when planning a trip. FilmStalker removes the usual hassle and plans your trip for you in no time.

What makes us different?

FilmStalker allows you to select your favourite movie, find the filming locations then shows you travel options. By car and by plane.
